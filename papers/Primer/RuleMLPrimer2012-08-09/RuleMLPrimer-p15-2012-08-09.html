RuleML Slots - Notes

</slot>: A user-defined slot consisting of a name (first position) and a filler (second position).

<resl>: A slotted rest variable used in <Atom>s, <Expr>s and <Plex>s. Note that <Plex>s are generated, so <resl>s are only available above the Datalog sublanguage.
