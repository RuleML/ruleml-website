Backward Chaining Derivation

  1. Goal succeeds if both conjuncts of antecedent succeed.
    • Subgoal: "Peter Miller is premium."
    • Subgoal: "A Porsche is a luxury product."
  2. Both subgoals succeed.
    • "Peter Miller is premium" succeeds by chaining.
    • "A Porsche is a luxury product." succeeds by fact.
