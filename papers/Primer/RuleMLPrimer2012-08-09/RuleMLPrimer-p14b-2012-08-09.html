RuleML Queries with variables - Notes

</Query>:A performative acting as a wrapper specifying that its content (optionally surrounded by a <formula> role) is queried, making an 'implicit <Rulebase>' assumption. This allows the separation of declarative content from such procedural performatives (as pioneered by KQML, which has a similar performative 'ask'). .
