Validation Instructions for RuleML 0.85 Examples (using DTDs)
-------------------------------------------------------------
Last modified: December 18, 2003


1. Direct your browser to http://www.stg.brown.edu/service/xmlvalid/
(STG XML Validation Form).

2. Enter the URL of the an example RuleML file into the textfield labelled
"URI:".

e.g. http://www.ruleml.org/0.85/exa/urcbindatagroundfact.ruleml

3. Click the "Validate" button.


Note: The validation may take a while, and numerous warnings will be reported,
depending on which DTD is being used.
