Overview

ECA-RuleML is an ECA Rule Markup Language for the serialization and interchange of reaction rules and event / action logics.  Historically, it has been developed in the Rule Based Service Levele Agreement project RBSLA: http://ibis.in.tum.de/research/projects/rbsla/ and is part of the RBSLA language.

ECA-RuleML since version 0.3 has been merged in the Reaction RuleML 0.1 and will be further developed in the Reaction RuleML project: http://reaction.ruleml.org/
