Conclusions

  • RuleML DTD 0.7, a system of 12 DTDs, is available at http://www.ruleml.org/indtd.html; sample files at http://www.ruleml.org/exa

  • The rule representation in RDF is described at http://www.ruleml.org/inrdf.html

  • Further rule categories (e.g. ICs and triggers) and DTD updates will be available via main RuleML page at http://www.dfki.de/ruleml

  • Distributed KR can already be based on current DTDs -- using (XSLT) transformations to reach follow-up and Participants’ DTDs
