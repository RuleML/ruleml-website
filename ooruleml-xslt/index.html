Introduction

As Object-Oriented RuleML has been developed, the need for translators to and from Object-Oriented RuleML has become apparent. This site contains current translators pairs to and from Object-Oriented RuleML. If you have developed any translators dealing with Object-Oriented RuleML please contact Harold Boley to share your work.
